# Google AdSense Eligibility & Action Plan

This document outlines the requirements, current status, and necessary steps to successfully apply for and monetize Toolisiya.com using Google AdSense.

## 1. AdSense Eligibility Requirements
To be approved for Google AdSense, a website must meet the following strict criteria:
- **Age:** The website should ideally be active for at least 6 months (though high-quality new sites can sometimes be approved faster).
- **Original Content:** The site must provide unique, valuable content (not just aggregated links or scraped tools).
- **Policy Compliance:** Absolutely no adult content, malware, copyrighted material, or illegal activities.
- **Navigation:** Clear, user-friendly navigation with no broken links.
- **Mandatory Pages:** A dedicated **Privacy Policy**, **Terms of Service**, and **Contact Us** page must exist and be easily accessible.
- **Traffic:** While there is no official minimum, sites typically need sufficient organic traffic (often 10k+ monthly pageviews) to be deemed worthwhile for advertisers.

## 2. Current Website Status Assessment
Toolisiya.com is strongly positioned for AdSense approval:
- **Strengths:** 
  - The site offers a comprehensive suite of 40+ highly functional, original tools.
  - The UI/UX is exceptionally clean, modern, and mobile-responsive.
  - Navigation is clearly structured via categories and sticky headers.
- **Weaknesses / Missing Items:**
  - Need to ensure that the actual `/privacy-policy` and `/terms-of-service` pages contain comprehensive legal text specifically addressing cookies and third-party tracking (crucial for AdSense).
  - High-quality text content is needed on tool pages (e.g., "How to use", "FAQs") to satisfy Google's "valuable text content" requirement, as tools alone (just code/calculators) are sometimes flagged as "thin content".

## 3. Action Plan (What Needs To Be Done)
Before submitting your application to Google AdSense, complete the following checklist:

1. **Update Legal Pages:**
   - Update the Privacy Policy to include a specific clause about **Google AdSense and DoubleClick cookies** (stating that third-party vendors use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits).
   - Ensure the Terms of Service and Contact pages are fully populated and linked in the footer.

2. **Add Supporting Text Content to Tools:**
   - AdSense bots look for text. Ensure every tool page utilizes the `CalculatorLayout`'s description, formula, example, and FAQ sections.
   - Aim for at least 300 words of descriptive, helpful text on the most popular tool pages.

3. **Verify Compliance:**
   - Run a dead-link checker to ensure zero 404 errors.
   - Verify that the site has no pop-ups that obscure content or force clicks.

## 4. Timeline for Eligibility
- **If the site is brand new:** Focus on SEO and traffic building for the next 2-3 months. Add the text content mentioned above.
- **If the site already has organic traffic:** Complete the Action Plan and apply immediately.
- **Review Process:** Once applied, Google's review process typically takes **2 to 4 weeks**. They will review the site automatically via bots and then manually via human reviewers.

## 5. How to Apply
1. Go to [google.com/adsense](https://www.google.com/adsense/start/).
2. Click **Get Started** and sign in with your primary Google account.
3. Enter your website URL (`toolisiya.com`).
4. Select your country and accept the Terms and Conditions.
5. Click **Start using AdSense**.
6. Follow the verification steps: Google will provide a code snippet. Add this script tag inside the `<head>` of your `apps/web/index.html` file.
7. Click **Request review** in the AdSense dashboard and wait for the approval email.
